The Canned Ham

FEATURED ARTICLE

The Canned Ham is stewdio project D!Y BnB located in the yard of The Silent Barn (( silentbarn.org ))

Located only one block from the Myrtle-Broadway stop on J/M/Z train! Less than 20 minutes to Manhattan, Williamsburg and Queens!

The Canned Ham is a 10’ x 6’ vintage 1972 Shasta “canned ham” style travel trailer with a sink, oven/stove, heater/ac, storage, electric hookup and sleeping room for up to 3 people. Bathrooms, showers, fresh linens are all available! Wake up at your leisure and use the Canned Ham kitchen to whip up some breakfast before continuing your journey!
